About me

Hello! I'm Travis Huynh from Vietnam. A data-driven problem solver with a keen eye for uncovering insights and transforming raw information into impactful solutions.

My journey into data analytics began with a strong foundation in business strategy and management, developed during my Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration. As I pursued my Master of Science in Global Supply Chain Management, I found my passion for using data to optimize operations, improve efficiency, and drive strategic decisions.

Working at VinFast Vietnam and ADL Trade & Service Co., LTD revealed my enthusiasm for data management and leading team operations. I collaborated with engineers, analysts, and stakeholders, ensuring data accuracy, structuring complex datasets, and solving problems with an analytical mindset. These experiences solidified my belief that data is not just numbers—it’s a powerful tool for innovation and business growth.

To further develop my expertise, I immersed myself in learning Python, SQL, Power BI, and Excel, mastering the ability to turn messy data into clear, actionable insights. My adaptability allows me to thrive in dynamic environments, continuously evolving to meet the ever-changing landscape of data and analytics. Whether it’s business intelligence or strategic decision-making, I’m excited about the potential of data to shape industries and solve real-world challenges.

Let’s connect and explore how we can transform data into meaningful insights! Reach out via LinkedIn or check out my work on GitHub—I’d love to discuss ideas and collaborate.
